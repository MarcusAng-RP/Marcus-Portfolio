Song Rating App

The app allowing users to input song details such as the song name, singer, year of release, and their personal rating for each song. Upon clicking the "Insert" button, a toast message confirms the successful addition of the song.

The app also features a "Show List" button, enabling users to view all the added songs in a separate activity. Within this activity, users have the ability to filter songs based on their ratings, specifically allowing them to display songs with a 5-star rating.

Furthermore, the list of songs in the new activity provides interactive functionality. Users can click on individual songs to access options such as "Update" or "Delete." The "Update" option allows users to modify the song's details, while the "Delete" option removes the selected song from the list. This provides users with comprehensive control over the song database within the app
