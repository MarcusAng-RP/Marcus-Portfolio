Typography

This is a typography art that I made manipulating the the the angles of the word and changing them into different form. With the manipulation, I am able to form up a portrait of a person.

Inspiration

The portrait of the person is inspire by the person that created the garamond font, Claude Garamond. I felt that It would be interesting to form up his portrait using the font that he created. The art can be used to showcase both the font and the creator.

Made with

Photoshop
