Simple Escape The Lab

The VP game begins with the user spawning in a science lab room featuring two machines resembling vending machines. Each machine is equipped with three buttons: Red, Green, and Blue. Players are tasked with selecting one button on each machine. To successfully escape, players must press the correct button on the first machine followed by the correct button on the second machine, adhering to this specific sequence for a successful escape.

Game Locomotion

The game uses a square area one the floor. When the hovers around the floor, places that cna be navigated would change into blue color. Press the trigger button to teleport to the area. This form of locomotion allow for inclusivity , as player do not need to move around physically.
