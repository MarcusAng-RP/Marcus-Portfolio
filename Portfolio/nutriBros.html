NutriBro Recipe App (TeamWork)

Our team has developed a recipe app dedicated to promoting healthy eating habits. This innovative platform enables users to browse and select various recipes. Each recipe is accompanied by a comprehensive video tutorial that guides users through the cooking process. Additionally, the app provides essential nutritional information for each dish or drink, empowering users to make informed choices about their diet and overall health.
